I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn since January 2022. My mentor is Prof. Dr. Peter Scholze. I received my Ph.D. from the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai in July 2016, under the guidance of Prof. Amalendu Krishna.

Broadly, my area of research interest is algebraic geometry. I am particularly interested in algebraic K-theory and algebraic cycles in the context of algebraic stacks and equivariant algebraic geometry. I am currently working on problems related to cohomology of algebraic stacks.
