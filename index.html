I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn since January 2022. Before this from September 2018 - December 2021, I was an SFB Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Regensburg in the research group of Prof. Dr. Ulrich Bunke. Before that from 2016 - 2018, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Oslo working in the Motivic Hopf equations project under the supervision of Prof. Paul Arne Østvær. I received my Ph.D. from the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai in July 2016, under the guidance of Prof. Amalendu Krishna.

Broadly, my area of research interest is algebraic geometry. I am particularly interested in algebraic K-theory and algebraic cycles in the context of algebraic stacks and equivariant algebraic geometry. I am currently working on problems related to cohomology of algebraic stacks.
